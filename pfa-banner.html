Mental Health in Singapore

Why Psychological First Aid Matters Now

In Singapore, many people experiencing anxiety or depression do not seek professional help. Barriers such as stigma, fear of judgement, privacy concerns, and workplace worries can make it difficult for people to speak up.

Support does not always begin in a clinic. It can begin with a colleague, friend, family member, or peer who notices something is wrong and knows how to respond.

77%

of adults with anxiety or depression symptoms had not sought professional help.

62%

were open to receiving support from peers with similar lived experiences.
